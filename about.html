About

Pablo Orta writes, directs and edits animated stories for global screens.

International Emmy® Best Series Kids / Factual
50+ festivals Local and international selections
8 years Creative Director and Editor at Mighty Animation

An award-winning creator hailing from Guadalajara, Mexico, Pablo Orta wrote, directed, and edited The Secret Life of Your Mind, the winner of the International Emmy® Award for Best Series in the Kids Factual category. The show premiered on HBO Max LATAM in late 2023.

Pablo Orta has showcased his films at over 50 local and international film festivals. His debut feature film, Goya, won the titles of Best Director and Best Film at the Oklahoma Latino Film Festival, in addition to securing the Audience Prize at FICG 37.

Over the last eight years, he has served as the Creative Director and Editor at Mighty Animation, collaborating with clients including Cartoon Network, Disney, Lucasfilm, Netflix, and Nickelodeon.
