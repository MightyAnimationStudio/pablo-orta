The Secret Life
of Your Mind

★ International Emmy® Award 2024 ★ Best Series — Kids Factual HBO Max Original World Premiere 2023

Do you find it challenging to understand why you think or behave the way you do? Don't worry — your mind can be a complicated place. Discover, alongside Ceri, its secrets, and unravel the greatest mystery of the universe: why you are the way you are.

The series premiered on HBO Max LATAM in late 2023 and won the 2024 International Emmy® Award for Best Series in the Kids Factual category.
