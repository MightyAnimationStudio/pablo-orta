Feature Film · IMCINE / FOPROCINE

Goya

★ Best Director — Oklahoma Cine Latino ★ Best Film — Oklahoma Cine Latino ★ Best Script — Ensenada IFF ★ Best Mexican Feature — Ensenada IFF Audience Prize — FICG 37

Cesar and Mateo, two brothers struggling with financial problems, try to save their unknown neighbor's ill and abandoned dog Goya despite only having a few days of joy left together. Together with their friend and neighbor Camila, they start a journey to rescue Goya from her tragic fate.
