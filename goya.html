Goya

Cesar and Mateo, two brothers struggling with financial problems, try to save their unknown neighbor's ill and abandoned dog Goya despite only having a few days of joy left together. Together with their friend and neighbor Camila, they start a journey to rescue Goya from her tragic fate. The brothers have to face difficult decisions that will decide the future of their family.
